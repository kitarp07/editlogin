How do i order a pet or product?

You can order a pet or product by signing in and
clicking add to cart and checktout button.


Can I visit the store in person to see the pets?

Yes, you can visit the store phusically to see the pets.


How do I pay for my purchase?

You can pay for the purchase on delivery or in the store itself.

Can I return the pets?

No, you cannot return pets once purchased.


How can i vaccinate my pets?

You can vaccinate your pets by clicking on services tab and
clicking vaccination where you should fill out the form.
Then you can visit the store at an appointed date.
